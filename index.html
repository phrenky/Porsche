The history of the new brand began long before the company was founded in 1931. In 1898, being only a 23-year-old enthusiast, Ferdinand Porsche created the first electric car. In 1900, working at Lohner & Co., he introduced the first hybrid car. In 1906 the engineer moved to Daimler, where he introduced the iconic models: S, SS and SSK.
